Phoenix Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Phoenix Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Phoenix Chamber of Commerce aims to:

  • Provide resources and support for local businesses to thrive.
  • Foster community engagement and promote local events.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be having this month that promote business networking?
  • How can I find local busineses that are members of the chamber?
  • What resources and counseling is available for startups in the Phoenix area?

Color Schema

The color palette is inspired by the culture and colors of Phoenix:

  • Desert Sand (#E9CBA7) - Used for background elements.
  • Cactus Green (#3A5D24) - Used for headers and buttons.

Typography

We will use the following fonts:

  • Heading Font: Montserrat - for all headings.
  • Body Font: Open Sans - for body text.
