Rental Agreement Information

By making a reservation, you agree to the following terms:

  • A $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations.

  • No one under the age of 12 is allowed to ride on scooters.
  • Fuel charges are not included in the rental price.
  • Liability insurance is included; damage insurance is available for purchase.
  • Unlimited mileage and free delivery to Cozumel hotels or Airbnbs for rentals of 2+ days.
